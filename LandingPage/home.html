Did you know?
The average family can waste 180 gallons per week, or 9,400 gallons of water annually, from household leaks. That's equivalent to the amount of water needed to wash more than 300 loads of laundry. Household leaks can waste approximately nearly 900 billion gallons of water annually nationwide.

Here are some ways you can stop wasting water!
Turn off the tap!

Turn off the tap when you're brushing your teeth. You can save 8 gallons by doing so every morning and night and 200 gallons a month.

Source
Fill your sink when shaving!

When washing or shaving, partially fill the sink and use that water rather than running the tap continuously. (This saves about 60% of the water normally used.)

Source
Don't constantly water your lawn!

Water lawns every three to five days, rather than for a short period every day. In warm weather, apply 5 millimetres of water for each day since the last watering.

Source
About
What is this website about? We want to promote the idea of water conservation and motivate individuals to use their water more efficiently. Through our website, users can compete to see who conserves the most water using their own data! Users fill out forms asking about their various water usage and are judged based on how low it is. By creating an atmosphere of competition, we hope it pushes for more water conservation and brings awareness to the signficance of preserving water.
